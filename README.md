\## Welcome



Welcome to \*\*ICE Operating System (ICE\_OS)\*\*.



ICE\_OS is the strategic knowledge repository that governs the design, evolution and implementation of the \*\*ICE™ Business Framework\*\*, a methodology created to help business owners regain control of their companies through management principles, business analytics and Artificial Intelligence.



This repository is not a software project.



It is the \*\*operating system of the company\*\*.



Every idea, decision, document, methodology, product definition and strategic evolution of ICE™ begins here.



\---



\# Our Purpose



We believe that most companies do not fail because they lack software.



They struggle because their leaders lack clarity, visibility and timely information to make better decisions.



Enterprise software records operations.



ICE™ helps transform those operations into better management decisions.



That is our mission.

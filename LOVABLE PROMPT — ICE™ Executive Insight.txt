# LOVABLE PROMPT — ICE™ Executive Insight Card v1.0

## Mission

Design and implement a premium floating component called **Executive Insight Card™**.

This component represents the first public interaction with the ICE™ platform.

ICE™ is an executive intelligence layer that complements ERP systems by helping business owners transform operational data into better business decisions.

The component must not feel like advertising.

It must feel like discovering something valuable.

---

## User Context

Visitors arrive at this website looking for ERP implementation services (SAINT ERP).

Many are business owners.

Many are managers.

Many already have ERP software.

Most believe their problem is technology.

Our mission is to make them realize their real challenge is business control and decision making.

---

## UX Goals

The component should create curiosity.

Not urgency.

Not pressure.

Not sales.

Visitors should think:

"I've never looked at my business that way."

---

## Component Type

Floating Executive Card

Never use:

* Modal
* Popup
* Full-screen overlay
* Aggressive banner

---

## Display Rules

Wait approximately 8 seconds after page load.

Show only if the visitor has started interacting with the page.

Do not interrupt immediately.
